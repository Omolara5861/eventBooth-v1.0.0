International Women’s Day 2024

  • 4:00 pm - 11:00 pm (Europe/Dublin)
  • March 16, 2024
  • Cavan Crystal Hotel County Cavan

We are delighted to extend an invitation to you for the International Women’s Day Celebration, taking place on March 16, 2024, at the CAVAN Crystal Hotel starting at 6:00 PM.

The International Women’s Day 2024 theme is #inspireinclusion (Inspire Inclusion).
Topic theme: ‘Digit All Innovation and technology for gender equality’

This year, we are proudly dedicating the event to the celebration of African Caribbean Women in Diaspora.

Highlight: “The Future: Illuminating Africa’s Past, Present & Future Through History, Art, Culture, and Exhibition”

As we come together to honor International Women’s Day, we recognize the invaluable contributions of African Caribbean women who have significantly impacted communities across the globe. This event aims to celebrate their achievements, resilience, and the rich cultural tapestry they bring to our society.

Join us for an evening filled with inspiration, empowerment, and cultural exchange. Your presence will contribute to making this celebration even more meaningful. Together, let us acknowledge and appreciate the strength and diversity of African Caribbean women in diaspora.
