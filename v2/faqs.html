Frequently Asked Questions

General

EventBooth is a comprehensive event management solution that empowers event organizers, vendors, and attendees to seamlessly plan, promote, and experience events of all types.

EventBooth provides a user-friendly platform where event organizers can list events, sell tickets, manage event merchandise, and engage with vendors. Attendees can discover, purchase tickets, and seamlessly validate their entry using QR codes.

EventBooth offers event listing, ticket sales and validation, event merchandise sale and vendor listing features. It streamlines the entire event lifecycle, from planning to execution.
